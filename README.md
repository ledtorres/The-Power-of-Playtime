# Key Insights
- FromSoftware's amazing player retention and completion defy difficulty expectations.
- Call of Duty's has a dying model for the fans but sales say otherwise.
- The Ubisoft formula creates consistency but players grow tired of it.
- The Goldilocks Zone sits at 5-15 hours for maximum retention and commercial success.
- 2016, 2020, 2022 had the biggest differences between critic and user scores.
